# Minecraft Music Mod

## Elevate Your Minecraft Experience with Music

Welcome to the Minecraft Music Mod, where your adventures are accompanied by enchanting melodies and custom tunes. Immerse yourself in the rhythmic ambiance of the Minecraft world like never before!

## Installation Guide

Embark on your musical journey by following these steps:

1. Begin by immersing yourself in the Minecraft universe with the latest version of Minecraft Forge 1.20.1. You can acquire this from the [official Forge website](https://files.minecraftforge.net/).
2. Embrace the power of customization by installing Minecraft Forge. Simply execute the downloaded installer and choose the "Install Client" option to commence your journey.
3. Elevate your gameplay with the Minecraft Music Mod. Discover the harmonious melodies it offers by obtaining the latest version from the [releases page](https://github.com/GunterPro7/MusicBoxes/releases).
4. Assemble the pieces of your musical masterpiece by placing the downloaded mod file into the "mods" folder of your Minecraft directory.

## Features

- Play your favorite Minecraft music discs at jukeboxes throughout your world.
- Customize your experience by adding your own sounds using the "/musicBox" command.
